Centre Stage (Shanti)

Sandwiches, Fries, Coffee

Want to eat something delicious? Somewhere peaceful, away from all the noise and crowd? Located just behind Aahar and Rasoi, Shanti is the perfect place to chill and hang out. This is the only café which offers a beautiful view. Grab their delicious hummus and pita and iced mocha and relax on the stairs near the cricket ground. A good time is guaranteed!

Things you'll need to know:

Timings: 9:00AM to 9:00PM (closes at 8:30PM on Sundays.)

Days operational: All days.

No deliveries.

FF Recommendations:

  • Quesadillas
  • Hummus pita
  • Loaded fries
  • Cold iced mocha
