JUICE PRESS

Tea, Paranthas, SPDP

Juice press is one of the best cafés in flame university and there are many reasons for that. From unique combinations like kurkure chat to simple food like maggie, they have it ALL and mind you, they have THE BEST maggie on campus and if you say otherwise, you would probably be lying. The location makes it more cosy as it doesn’t cater to many people. We understand that all of us have a budget and the best part is, juice press is one of the cheapest cafés on campus!

Things you'll need to know:

Timings: 9:30AM to 6:30PM

Days operational: Monday to Saturday.

No Delivery.
