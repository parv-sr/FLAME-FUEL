THE MESS

University-run mess, which provides fresh foods seven days a week. Although students give mixed reviews, it always has something for someones liking.

The Flame University mess serves a variety of meals with attention to different dietary preferences. Separate counters provide Jain and non-Jain food, ensuring that students can enjoy meals in line with their dietary restrictions. Additionally, there is a dedicated counter for non-spicy food for those who prefer milder flavors. There are two buildings, "Aahar" and "Rasoi." Rasoi, being the newer of the two, boasts better lighting and air conditioning, offering a more comfortable dining experience. The arrangement ensures that students can find meals tailored to their tastes and preferences in a well-organized environment.
