THE MESS

University-run mess, which provides fresh foods seven days a week. Although students give mixed reviews, it always has something for someones liking.

Had a long day? Just finished a crazy game? Or maybe you skipped dinner at Aahar or Rasoi because, let's be honest, walking all the way there felt like a marathon? Well, no worries, because the food truck has your back! Parked conveniently near the West Block (or "South Block" as the seniors call it), this little gem is your go-to for delicious, filling food at student-friendly prices. Whether you're starving or need a tasty snack, the food truck is here to rescue your taste buds and fill your stomach, minus the long trek!

Things you'll need to know:

Timings:

  1. Breakfast: 7:30AM - 9:30AM
  2. Lunch: 12:00PM - 3:00PM
  3. Evening snacks: 5:00PM - 6:00PM
  4. Dinner: 7:30PM - 9:30PM

Days operational: All days.

FF Recommendations:

  1. Pizza Pinwheel
  2. Paneer butter masala
  3. Rajma
  4. Chole Punjabi
